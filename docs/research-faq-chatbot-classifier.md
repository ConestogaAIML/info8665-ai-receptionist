### Hayati, R., Buaton, R., & Ramadani, S. (2024)

**Title:** Implementation of Chatbot Artificial Intelligence in a Company Website to Improve Customer Service Automatically Using the TF-IDF Method

**Journal:** Journal of Artificial Intelligence and Engineering Applications (JAIEA), Vol. 4(1), pp. 131–136

**DOI:** [https://doi.org/10.59934/jaiea.v4i1.584](https://doi.org/10.59934/jaiea.v4i1.584)

**URL:** [https://ioinformatic.org/index.php/JAIEA/article/view/584](https://ioinformatic.org/index.php/JAIEA/article/view/584)

**Published:** October 2024

**Summary:**
Develops and deploys an AI chatbot for a company website using TF-IDF to extract key features from customer questions and match them to the most relevant answers in a database. Reports significant improvement in response speed and customer satisfaction. The architecture — extract features from stored questions, classify incoming queries, return best-match answer — directly mirrors the design used in this project.

**Key findings:**

- TF-IDF effectively captures distinguishing terms in short customer queries
- The chatbot reduced manual handling time and improved first-response accuracy
- TF-IDF + cosine similarity or classifier matching is appropriate for company FAQ datasets

**Relevance to this project:** Direct match. A business website chatbot using TF-IDF to answer FAQs from a database is identical to the `BusinessFAQ` model and the `/chat` endpoint design.
